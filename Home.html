Trending Destinations

Popular Destination for Honeymoon

Now for some serene intimate getaway. Ella, a lovely Sri Lankan mountain village, is a place where the freshly hitched can just be, which makes it one of the best places to visit in Sri Lanka for honeymoon. Be sure to travel by train to this pretty place. Around you, there are acres of tea plantations, cloud covers, jungle canopies, and plenty of things to do in Ella that will keep you hooked to this place. Nestle yourselves cozily into this pristine setting. Ella has to be one of the most romantic Sri Lanka honeymoon destinations.

Did you know s?

Kandy, Nuwara Eliya, and Ella are 3 most eye-catching paradises in Sri Lanka. Once you visit this place you can enjoy hiking climbing or a plain, boat rides at lakes, taste a delicious cup of tea while watching Ceylon tea factories, visit many historical as well as religious places and you can have a fabulous bath in a waterfall. Did you know that you can have all these by visiting in place of the above?

Fabulous hotel at Kandy has been opened

Another fabulous hotel at Kandy has been opened. It is a wonderland with all the facilities, comfort, and an eye-catching surrounding near the Kandy lake by the name “Haweslake Hotel”. Visit Haweslake to have a special day.

A special occasion is opening in the Kandy lake

A special occasion is opening in the Kandy lake, it nothing else but seaplanes to have a ride around Kandy. When you are having a ride, you can see most of the eye-catching green pastures, forests, waterfalls, and one of the most sacred place the Temple of Tooth Relic in Kandy from the sky.

THE KANDY PERAHERA

The most colorful, sacred, and the religious procession celebrated by the Sri Lankans. “The Kandy procession” started in June and ends in July. In this, an elephant named ‘Raja’ carries the sacred tooth relic of the Lord Buddha on his back. Drummers, dancers, etc. join the procession to make it more beautiful and meaningful.

special train is running from Colombo to Kandy

During the Kandy Procession, a special train is running from Colombo to Kandy. It begins the journey from Colombo at 9.30 AM and arrives at Kandy at 1.00 PM and then it approaches from Kandy the next morning at 9.00 AM. This will make the journey more easy and comfortable.
